About Caloocan City Medical Center

Welcome to Caloocan City Medical Center, your trusted healthcare partner. We are dedicated to providing high-quality medical services to the community.

Our state-of-the-art facilities and compassionate healthcare professionals ensure that you receive personalized care tailored to your needs. From routine check-ups to specialized treatments, we are committed to your well-being.

For appointments and inquiries, please use our online appointment system or contact our front desk. Your health is our priority, and we look forward to serving you with excellence.
