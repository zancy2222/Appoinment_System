Services

Emergency Department

Open 24/7 to provide immediate medical attention. Our highly-trained emergency team handles various cases, from minor injuries to life-threatening conditions.

Pharmacy Services

Our in-house pharmacy offers quality and affordable medicines. Staffed by licensed pharmacists to assist patients with their medication needs.

Outpatient Services

For patients not requiring hospitalization, including general consultations, laboratory tests, and outpatient medical procedures.

Radiology Department

State-of-the-art radiology services, including X-rays, CT scans, and ultrasounds. Staffed by licensed radiologic technologists and board-certified radiologists.

Physical Therapy

Physical therapy services for rehabilitation after injury or surgery. Our licensed physical therapists design personalized treatment plans to help patients regain strength and mobility.

Specialized Medical Services

Various departments specializing in internal medicine, dermatology, orthopedics, and women's health, among others. Customized services to meet specific medical needs.

Travel Clinic

Visit our travel clinic for necessary vaccinations and medical advice before your trip. Our team provides information on health risks and recommends precautions for a healthy journey.

Dental Services

Comprehensive dental services for patients of all ages. Our licensed dentists offer routine check-ups and a wide range of dental treatments.
